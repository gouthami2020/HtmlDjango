Empowering Farmers

About us

The main goal of FarmOne mobile application is to help farmers in day to day farm related activities.
This app will act as an assistant to farmer and will give guidance when required.

Products & Services

Rent Equipment
This section is specific to organic methods of cultivation. It will have list of organic fertilizers, pesticides and also has details of organic suppliers.
My Farm
A customized feature where user will have details of their own farm. Everything in this section will be based on the details user provided about their crop. Push notification will be sent by analyzing weather forecast, market situation for the crop etc. Farmer will select the state of the farm (crop health, status of crop) on regular basis and get guidance on next step. (Multi crops by add button)
Farm Knowledge
Provides overall scientific information and cultivation methods for a given crop starting from farm preparation, types of seed varieties, sowing, weeding, common diseases, pest control, harvesting and marketing
Farm Forum
Open forum where all the app users can post a question or answer to the already posted question. App moderator will actively engage with the users in this section to help clarify any open question. All posts (questions and answers) will have “Like” and “Report” buttons. User will get farm points based on number of likes they receive. These points will accumulate and can be redeemed after reaching certain mark. User will get free voucher/gift cards to buy seeds/fertilizers/pesticides from a selected brand.
